Tale of the Red Cliffs

Experience one of the legendary battles for the Three Kingdoms in Tale of the Red Cliffs.

Select copper plates and shields and reveal matching symbols to win epic prizes! Watch out for fire arrows - when a symbol is hit by a fire arrow, a jade multiplier symbol may be revealed in the ashes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Tale of the Red Cliffs is to match YOUR SYMBOLS to any WINNING SYMBOLS.

Select each copper plate to reveal a WINNING SYMBOL. Select each shield to reveal a YOUR SYMBOL and a prize value. After all symbols are revealed, fire arrows may strike YOUR SYMBOL and burn the symbol to reveal a jade multiplier with a value of 2x to 20x. Press REVEAL ALL to have the game play to completion automatically.

When YOUR SYMBOL matches a WINNING SYMBOL, the YOUR SYMBOL prize value is awarded.

Reveal a jade symbol with a multiplier to win the YOUR SYMBOL’s prize value times the multiplier.

Play ends when all copper plates and shields have been revealed. A message appears indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all items have been revealed.

Press STOP to continue the game in manual play mode.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
